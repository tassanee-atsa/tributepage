King Chulalongkorn

The King who modernised Thailand

Overview

King Chulalongkorn was crowned king as Rama V on 16 November 1873. He was the fifth King of the Chakri dynasty. He is known for modernising and transforming Thailand from a Madala system to the current districts and borders of today's united Thailand. He was the first King of Siam to visit Europe and brought many western practices into Thailand.

Here are some of the achievements of King Chulalongkorn

  • Introduced the system of Monthons which created administrative districts and solidified Thailand's borders.
  • Abolished slavery and the corvee system in Thailand.
  • Opened the first Railway route in Thailand in 1901.
  • Maintained Thailand's independence despite being surrounded by colonised countries.
